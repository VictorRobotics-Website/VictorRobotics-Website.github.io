About Us

Team 1559, Devil Tech, was founded by Peter Fleckenstein and a group of passionate students in 2004. We are a FIRST robotics team that focuses on student growth and development. We strive to balance the mentor/student development of all aspects of the team - our robot is built by students, aided by mentors.
